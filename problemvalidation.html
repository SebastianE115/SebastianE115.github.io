Market Analysis 1

  • Citation: Marques Pereira, P., Schneider, A., Pannetier, S. et al. Coffin–Lowry syndrome. Eur J Hum Genet 18, 627–633 (2010). https://doi.org/10.1038/ejhg .2009.189

  • Description: Coffin–Lowry syndrome is an X-linked syndrome characterized typically by severe psychomotor and growth retardation, facial dysmorphism, digit abnormalities, and progressive skeletal changes. It tends to vary greatly between individuals as there are differences in the severity and expressions of many patients.

  • Critique: This artifact provides a good description on how Coffin-Lowry syndrome is caused and the symptoms of the disease. As well as going into different studies and tests that have been done. This artifact allows us to understand what is best for our client.
