RFP

Requirements

  • Critical Requirements

    • Drive Lockout

      • This would allow Jose to take control remotely without Jorge reseting the connection.

    • Steering Lockout

      • This will allow Jose to take control and not have to worry about Jorge breaking the steering wheel.

  • Optional Features

    • Custom Decals

      • This adds to the asthetic as well as providing information that the car is a service vehichle.

    • Keys

      • This allows Jorge to fidget and have fun with fake keys.

    • 3D Printed Accesories

      • Some examples of this are thorugh the steering column, etc.

    • Website

      • Provide a website with a step by step guide on how to add the attatchments.

    • RC Car Feature

      • Upgrade this feature by improving the remote.

    • Upgrading Parts

      • Increase efficiency and battery life.

    • Increasing Speed

      • This would allow for smoother rides.

    • Increase Radius

      • This allows Jose to have a clear mind knowing that he won't loose control of the car no matter the length.
